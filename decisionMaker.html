An app to help you make decisions!

Add options to the list one by one.

When you've got your list of options, you can narrow it down to two to decide between or have the program make a decision for you.

Clear the list whenever to start from scratch.

Don't like your results? Restore the original list and edit the list to remove those options before narrowing it down again!


Created by Raul Patel, a computer science student at the University of Oregon. Any suggestions? Check out my github!
